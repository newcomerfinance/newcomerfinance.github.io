Module 1: Your First Steps in Canada

Your essential financial checklist for the first few weeks. We'll guide you through getting an address, a phone plan, and opening your first bank account without the stress.

Module 2: Decoding Your Canadian Credit Score

Your credit score is a three-digit number from 300 to 900 that determines your financial opportunities. Learn the 5 key factors and which free apps to use to track your score.

Module 3: Banking 101: A Newcomer's Glossary

What's the difference between a Chequing and a Savings account? How do Interac e-Transfers really work? This module is your go-to dictionary for Canadian banking.

Learn actionable tips that work. We'll cover how to use a Secured Credit Card to your advantage and when and how to ask for credit limit increases to lower your utilization.

Did you know that some store loyalty cards are actually hidden credit cards? We teach you how to recognize these traps and how to identify phone and text scams targeting newcomers.
